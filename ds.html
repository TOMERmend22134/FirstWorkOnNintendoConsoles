Nintendo DS

The Nintendo DS is a handheld game console produced by Nintendo, released globally across 2004 and 2005. Nintendo DS is home to many famous games, such as New Super Mario Bros. DS, Pokémon Black and White, Mario Hoops 3-on-3 and more.

There are 3 models

There are 3 different models of the DS:
Nintendo DS, Nintendo 2DS and Nintendo 3DS.

DS

The Nintendo DS is a handheld game console produced by Nintendo, released globally across 2004 and 2005.

2DS

The Nintendo 2DS is a handheld game console produced by Nintendo which maintains otherwise identical hardware, similar functionality, and compatibility with software designed for the Nintendo DS and 3DS.

3DS

The Nintendo 3DS is a handheld game console produced by Nintendo. It was announced in March 2010 as the successor to the Nintendo DS.
